User Guide

Your comprehensive guide to BachatGuru

Getting Started

1
Download and install BachatGuru from the app store.
2
Create an account using your email for secure data backup.
3
Set your preferred currency and financial goals from the settings menu.

Adding Transactions

1
Tap the "+" button on the home screen to open the transaction menu.
2
Select whether it's an 'Income' or 'Expense'.
3
Enter the amount, choose a category, and add an optional description.
4
Tap 'Save' to record the transaction.

Managing Loans

1
Navigate to the 'Loans' section from the main menu.
2
Tap 'Add Loan' and specify whether you've given or received the loan.
3
Fill in the details, including the amount and the other party involved.

Generating Reports

1
Go to the 'Reports' section to see a summary of your finances.
2
Filter reports by time period (e.g., 7 days, 30 days, 1 year).
3
View detailed charts and graphs of your spending and earning patterns.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I manage loan records?

Navigate to the Loans section, tap "Add Loan" and fill in the required details. You can track both given and received loans.

How do I generate financial reports?

Go to the Reports section, select your preferred time period (7 days, 30 days, etc.), and view your customized financial analysis.

How secure is my financial data?

Your data is protected with end-to-end encryption and stored locally on your device. We prioritize your privacy and security.
